Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

TL;DR: Cookie Crusher does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or browsing history. Everything happens locally in your browser.

What Data We Collect

None. Cookie Crusher does not collect any personal information. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

How the Extension Works

  • Cookie Popup Detection: The extension scans web pages for known cookie consent popup patterns and removes them locally.
  • Statistics: Crush counts are stored locally in Chrome's sync storage, tied to your browser profile, not our servers.
  • Whitelist: Sites you whitelist are stored locally in Chrome's sync storage.

Pro License Verification

If you purchase Cookie Crusher Pro:

  • Your email address is sent to our server only when you click "Unlock" to verify your purchase.
  • We check if a valid license exists for that email.
  • We do NOT store browsing data, usage patterns, or any other personal information.
  • License data is stored securely and used solely for purchase verification.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: Payment processing is handled by Stripe. Their privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Supabase: License data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure.

Permissions Explained

  • Access all websites: Required to detect and remove cookie popups on any site you visit. We do NOT read or store your browsing history.
  • Storage: Used to save your preferences (enabled/disabled, whitelist) locally.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to breach. License verification uses HTTPS encryption.

Children's Privacy

Cookie Crusher is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
